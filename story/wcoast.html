West Coast is the Best Coast

After they became engaged, they explored wild tundra, ate tons of berries, and fell in love with Alaska. They relished in the opportunity to summit peaks, traverse glaciers, and encounter moose on a daily basis. They loved existing in a place which still feels wild and unexplored, despite some development. They plan on returning annually.

Murti is now pursuing a PhD at UC Berkeley, temporarily halting their nomadic lifestyle. While they yearn for the simple times with their beloved trailer, they are thriving in Oakland. They're thrilled to live in a place with a huge concentration of Ethiopian restaurants, and a wide range of ethnicities!
