An Uncertain Start

They indeed did see each other around.

They flew a Cessna-182 to Bryce Canyon and Murti was excited to make more friends who were backpacking and could lend her gear. Discovering the improbably-looking hoodoos, they learned about their mutual love of exploration, curiosity, and the wilderness. After going on fun hikes together, working on Parke's old Land Cruiser, they spent a weekend in a mountain cabin throwing caber, splitting wood, deadhanging, and eating delicious chili with mutual friends.
