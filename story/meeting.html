First Meeting

Parke and Murti were graduate and undergraduate students

working at the Laboratory for Atmospheric and Space Physics (LASP) at CU Boulder when they met in 2017. They co-existed in parallel on opposite sides of LASP's second floor until, one fortunate day, they met in the middle. Murti encountered Parke in the kitchen rinsing a dish. Ever the personable coworker, Murti immediately introduced herself.

Murti asked,

"are you that guy who wears the eyepatch?" The question had merit. Parke had just relinquished an eye patch he had been shamelessly wearing for the last year in a bid to train an amblyopic eye into astronaut-eligible acuity. With a charming chuckle, he responded, "Yep, that's me!" Mutually intrigued, they parted ways. "See you around!"
